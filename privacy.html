Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Channel Academy Step Tracker. This application is an internal tool developed for our company's health and wellness campaign. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Data We Collect

When you use our application and connect your Google Fit account, we only request access to the following data:

  • Step Count Data: We read your daily step count using the Google Fit API (https://www.googleapis.com/auth/fitness.activity.read).
  • Basic Profile Info: Your Name and Employee ID provided during registration.

3. How We Use Your Data

The data collected is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To calculate your daily total steps.
  • To display your name and steps on our internal company leaderboard.
  • To determine eligibility for internal health campaign rewards.

We do not use this data for targeted advertising, nor do we sell or share this data with any third-party marketing companies.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored in our internal Google Sheets database. Access is strictly limited to the campaign administrators.

5. Google API Services User Data Policy

Our application's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

6. How to Revoke Access

You can revoke our application's access to your Google Fit data at any time by visiting your Google Account settings: Google Account Permissions and removing access for "Channel Academy Step Tracker".

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact the Channel Academy & Communication admin team.
